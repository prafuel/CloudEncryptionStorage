A random line is a trial survey line that runs from a station toward a predetermined point that cannot be seen from the station. It avoids obstacles between stations. 
You can randomize text lines using these tools: 
Notepad++: Select the lines to randomize, then go to the NPP menu, Plugins, Python Script, Scripts, and RandomizeLines.
Browserling: Paste your text in the form, then press the Randomize Lines button.
IPVoid: Paste the text, then press the Randomize Lines button.
Online Random Tools: Paste the text, then click the Shuffle Lines button.
You can also find free graphic resources for random lines on Freepik
